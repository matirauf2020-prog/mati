Professional Introduction

Designing the Future of Solar Energy in Pakistan

I am a professional Solar PV Designer with hands-on expertise in designing and simulating photovoltaic systems for every scale — from small residential setups to multi-megawatt industrial power plants.

My work spans across all sectors: factory and warehouse solar projects, commercial buildings, hotels, and hospitals, and smart residential installations. I use industry-leading tools — PVsyst for energy simulation and SketchUp for 3D layout modeling — to deliver precise, bankable solar designs.

With 49+ completed projects documented on YouTube, my portfolio demonstrates a consistent record of professional-grade deliverables including PVsyst reports, SketchUp 3D layouts, Single Line Diagrams (SLD), and complete technical documentation.

What I Offer

Services

End-to-end solar PV design solutions with professional documentation for every project size and type.

Industrial Solar Layout Planning

Complete industrial PV design for factories, mills, and warehouses. Includes PVsyst simulation, SketchUp 3D layout, L2/L4/L5 structure planning, and full technical documentation.

Commercial Solar Design

Professional PV layouts for hotels, hospitals, schools, commercial buildings, and offices. Includes shade analysis, energy yield simulation, and structural planning.

Residential Solar Design

Smart home PV system design using P1–P4, L2, L5 structures. Optimized panel layout, roof-load consideration, and complete design package for installation teams.

PVsyst Energy Simulation

Accurate energy yield modeling, performance ratio calculation, near-shading analysis, and comprehensive PVsyst reports for project financing and approvals.

SLD & Technical Drawings

Single line diagrams, AC/DC cable sizing charts, protection device specifications, inverter wiring schematics, and installation-ready technical documentation.

Solar Carport Design

Elevated solar canopy and carport structures for commercial and industrial parking areas — balancing vehicle clearance requirements with maximum energy generation.
